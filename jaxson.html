What is Valorant?

Valorant is a first person shooter game with good graphics and fast pace combat. It is a 5v5 Attackers against Defenders.
Attackers have to plant a bomb called the Spike while defenders try to defuse it or finnsh off all of the attackers before they plant it.
There is a certain amount of time as to when to plant or defuse the bomb.
if the bomb goes off anybody in the radius will die but the next round will start soon.
