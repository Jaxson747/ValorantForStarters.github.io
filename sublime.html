Basics

Don't be bad. Always aim your crosshair (target) head level so you can aim for the head this is called crosshair placement. Don't position yourself where it could be a 1v2. You wont win a 1v2. Always be aware of where people are by looking at the mini map. Trade off teamates. If a teamate dies shoot the enemy from a different angle because the enemy will be looking at where your teamate died. If your location gets revealed reosition only if you can though.
